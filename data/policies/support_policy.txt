CARTLY E-COMMERCE PLATFORM — CUSTOMER SUPPORT POLICY
=====================================================

Last Updated: January 2025

1. OVERVIEW
-----------
Cartly's Customer Support team is available to assist with order issues, account queries, payments, returns, and any other platform-related concerns. This policy outlines support channels, response times, and escalation procedures.

2. SUPPORT CHANNELS
--------------------
Cartly offers support through the following channels:
- Live Chat: Available 24/7 on the Cartly app and website. First response within 2 minutes during peak hours.
- Email Support: support@cartly.in — Response within 24 hours on business days.
- Phone Support: 1800-CARTLY-0 (toll-free) — Mon–Sat, 8 AM – 10 PM IST.
- Help Center: help.cartly.in — Self-service FAQs, guides, and troubleshooting articles.
- Social Media: Responses via @CartlySupport on Twitter/X and Facebook within 4 hours.

3. RESPONSE TIME COMMITMENTS
-----------------------------
- Live Chat: Under 2 minutes for initial response.
- Email: Within 24 hours (business days).
- Phone: Average hold time under 3 minutes.
- Complex Issues: Resolution within 3–5 business days.

4. CARTLY PLUS PRIORITY SUPPORT
---------------------------------
Cartly Plus subscribers receive:
- Dedicated priority support queue.
- Average response time under 30 seconds on live chat.
- Dedicated phone line with no wait time.

5. ESCALATION PROCESS
----------------------
If your issue is not resolved at the first level:
- Request escalation to a senior agent during the interaction.
- Email escalations@cartly.in with your ticket number.
- Response from escalation team within 48 hours.

6. PROHIBITED CONDUCT
----------------------
Customers engaging in abusive, threatening, or inappropriate behavior toward support agents may have their support access temporarily suspended.

7. FEEDBACK
-----------
After every support interaction, customers receive a satisfaction survey. Responses help Cartly improve service quality.

8. CONTACT
----------
Email: support@cartly.in
Phone: 1800-CARTLY-0 (Mon–Sat, 8 AM – 10 PM IST)
