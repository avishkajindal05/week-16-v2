CARTLY E-COMMERCE PLATFORM — CANCELLATION POLICY
=================================================

Last Updated: January 2025

1. OVERVIEW
-----------
Customers may cancel orders placed on Cartly subject to the conditions outlined in this policy. Cancellations are time-sensitive, and the ability to cancel depends on the order's processing status.

2. CANCELLATION WINDOW
-----------------------
Orders can be cancelled free of charge within 1 hour of placement, regardless of payment method. After 1 hour:
- Orders still in "Processing" status: Can be cancelled, but a ₹20 cancellation convenience fee may apply.
- Orders in "Packed" or "Shipped" status: Cannot be directly cancelled. Customers must wait for delivery and then initiate a return.

3. HOW TO CANCEL
----------------
1. Go to "My Orders" in your Cartly account.
2. Select the order you wish to cancel.
3. Click "Cancel Order."
4. Select a cancellation reason from the dropdown.
5. Confirm the cancellation.

You will receive an email confirmation of the cancellation within 15 minutes.

4. REFUND AFTER CANCELLATION
------------------------------
- Prepaid orders (card/UPI/wallet): Refund issued to the original payment method within 3–5 business days.
- COD orders: No refund applicable as payment was not made.
- Wallet orders: Instant refund to the Cartly Wallet.

5. SELLER-INITIATED CANCELLATIONS
-----------------------------------
Occasionally, sellers may cancel your order due to stock unavailability or pricing errors. In such cases:
- You will be notified immediately via email and SMS.
- A full refund is processed automatically within 24 hours.
- Cartly may offer a substitute product suggestion or a discount coupon as compensation.

6. PARTIAL CANCELLATION
------------------------
For multi-item orders, individual items can be cancelled without affecting the rest of the order. The refund applies only to the cancelled item(s).

7. SUBSCRIPTION CANCELLATION
------------------------------
For Cartly Plus subscriptions, refer to the Subscription Policy for dedicated cancellation procedures.

8. NON-CANCELLABLE ORDERS
--------------------------
The following cannot be cancelled post-placement:
- Flash sale items once payment is confirmed.
- Custom or personalized orders once production has begun.
- Digital goods once download links are issued.
