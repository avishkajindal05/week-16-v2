CARTLY E-COMMERCE PLATFORM — REFUND POLICY
==========================================

Last Updated: January 2025

1. OVERVIEW
-----------
Cartly is committed to ensuring customer satisfaction. This Refund Policy outlines the conditions under which customers may request a refund for purchases made on the Cartly platform.

2. ELIGIBILITY FOR REFUNDS
---------------------------
Customers are eligible for a full refund under the following conditions:
- The item received is significantly different from the description on the product page.
- The item arrives damaged, defective, or non-functional.
- The order was not delivered within the guaranteed delivery window and no updated tracking is available.
- A duplicate charge was processed for the same order.

3. REFUND TIMELINE
------------------
Once a refund request is approved, the following timelines apply:
- Credit/Debit Card payments: Refunds are processed within 5–7 business days from approval.
- UPI and Net Banking: Refunds are processed within 3–5 business days.
- Cartly Wallet credits: Refunds are instant upon approval.
- EMI orders: Refunds follow the issuing bank's schedule, typically 7–10 business days.

4. NON-REFUNDABLE ITEMS
------------------------
The following categories are not eligible for refunds unless the item is defective:
- Perishable goods (food, flowers, plants).
- Personalized or custom-made items.
- Digital goods and software licenses once activated.
- Hygiene-sensitive products (undergarments, swimwear) once opened.

5. HOW TO INITIATE A REFUND
----------------------------
To initiate a refund:
1. Log in to your Cartly account.
2. Navigate to "My Orders."
3. Select the relevant order and click "Request Refund."
4. Choose the reason from the dropdown menu and upload supporting photos if applicable.
5. Submit the request. You will receive a confirmation email within 24 hours.

6. PARTIAL REFUNDS
------------------
Partial refunds may be issued in cases where:
- Only part of the order was affected (e.g., one item in a multi-item order was damaged).
- A discount coupon was used and the refund amount reflects the actual amount paid.

7. REFUND STATUS TRACKING
--------------------------
Customers can track refund status in real time by visiting:
Account → My Orders → Refund Status

Support escalations can be raised if the refund is not received within the stated timeline.

8. CHARGEBACKS
--------------
Cartly strongly advises customers to first raise a refund request directly on the platform before initiating a chargeback with their bank. Unauthorized chargebacks may result in temporary account suspension pending investigation.
