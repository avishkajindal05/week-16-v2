CARTLY E-COMMERCE PLATFORM — SUBSCRIPTION POLICY (CARTLY PLUS)
===============================================================

Last Updated: January 2025

1. OVERVIEW
-----------
Cartly Plus is a paid subscription program offering exclusive benefits to members. This policy covers membership plans, benefits, billing, and cancellation procedures.

2. CARTLY PLUS PLANS
---------------------
- Monthly Plan: ₹149/month.
- Annual Plan: ₹999/year (equivalent to ₹83/month — save 44%).
- Family Plan: ₹1,799/year for up to 4 members.

3. BENEFITS
-----------
Cartly Plus members enjoy:
- Free express delivery on all eligible orders (no minimum order value).
- Early access to flash sales (24-hour head start).
- Exclusive member-only coupons and offers.
- 2x reward points on every purchase.
- Priority customer support with a dedicated queue.
- Free gift wrapping on all orders.

4. BILLING
----------
Subscription fees are charged on the billing date (date of first subscription). For annual plans, the full amount is charged upfront. For monthly plans, billing recurs automatically each month. Billing occurs via the saved payment method on the account.

5. FREE TRIAL
-------------
New Cartly Plus members are eligible for a 30-day free trial. Credit card details are required for the trial but are not charged until the trial period ends. The trial can be cancelled at any time before the trial ends without any charges.

6. CANCELLATION
---------------
To cancel Cartly Plus:
1. Go to Account Settings > Cartly Plus > Manage Subscription.
2. Click "Cancel Subscription."
3. Confirm cancellation.

Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period. Benefits remain active until the end of the paid period. No prorated refunds are issued for mid-period cancellations.

7. AUTO-RENEWAL
----------------
Subscriptions auto-renew unless cancelled. Cartly sends a reminder email 7 days before renewal. Users may disable auto-renewal from Account Settings.
