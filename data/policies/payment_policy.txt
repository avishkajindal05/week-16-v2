CARTLY E-COMMERCE PLATFORM — PAYMENT POLICY
============================================

Last Updated: January 2025

1. OVERVIEW
-----------
Cartly accepts a wide range of payment methods to ensure a seamless checkout experience. This policy outlines supported payment modes, security practices, and payment failure handling.

2. ACCEPTED PAYMENT METHODS
-----------------------------
Cartly supports the following payment options:
- Credit Cards: Visa, MasterCard, American Express, RuPay.
- Debit Cards: All major Indian bank debit cards.
- Net Banking: 50+ Indian banks supported.
- UPI: Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, BHIM UPI, Amazon Pay UPI.
- Cartly Wallet: Pre-loaded wallet for instant checkout.
- EMI: No-cost and low-cost EMI available on select cards and products.
- Buy Now Pay Later (BNPL): Available through Simpl, ZestMoney, and LazyPay.
- Cash on Delivery (COD): Available for eligible orders up to ₹10,000.

3. PAYMENT SECURITY
-------------------
All transactions on Cartly are secured with:
- 256-bit SSL encryption.
- PCI-DSS compliant payment processing.
- Two-factor authentication (OTP) for card transactions via 3D Secure.
- Tokenization of saved card details — Cartly never stores raw card numbers.

4. CASH ON DELIVERY (COD)
--------------------------
COD is available for orders up to ₹10,000 with an additional ₹29 handling fee. COD is not available for:
- Digital products and subscriptions.
- Pre-order items.
- Orders shipping to remote zones.
Customers with a history of COD rejections may have this option restricted.

5. EMI OPTIONS
--------------
No-cost EMI is available for purchases above ₹3,000 on select credit cards. EMI tenure options: 3, 6, 9, 12, and 24 months. The product page will display EMI breakdowns. Bank processing fees may apply and are displayed transparently at checkout.

6. PAYMENT FAILURE HANDLING
-----------------------------
If a payment fails:
- The amount is automatically refunded to the source within 5–7 business days.
- You will receive an email and SMS confirmation of the failed transaction.
- Retry the payment from "My Orders → Failed Payments."
If the amount is debited but the order is not confirmed, it will auto-refund within 48 hours. You may contact support if it exceeds this period.

7. INVOICE
----------
A GST-compliant digital invoice is generated for every completed order and available in "My Orders → Invoice."
