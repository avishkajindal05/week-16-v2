CARTLY E-COMMERCE PLATFORM — PROMOTIONS & COUPONS POLICY
=========================================================

Last Updated: January 2025

1. OVERVIEW
-----------
Cartly regularly offers promotional discounts, coupon codes, and cashback offers to customers. This policy explains how promotions work, eligibility conditions, and applicable restrictions.

2. TYPES OF PROMOTIONS
-----------------------
- Coupon Codes: Alphanumeric codes applied at checkout for a flat or percentage discount.
- Cashback Offers: Cashback credited to Cartly Wallet after order delivery.
- Bank Offers: Discounts for specific credit/debit cards in partnership with banks.
- Flash Sale Prices: Time-limited discounts on select products.
- Referral Bonuses: Wallet credits for referring new users.
- First-Order Discounts: Special pricing for first-time buyers on the platform.

3. APPLYING A COUPON
---------------------
At checkout, enter the coupon code in the "Apply Coupon / Gift Card" field and click "Apply." Only one coupon code can be applied per order unless explicitly stated otherwise.

4. COUPON CONDITIONS
---------------------
Coupons are subject to:
- Minimum order value (displayed in the coupon terms).
- Category or product restrictions.
- One-time use per customer unless stated as "unlimited use."
- Validity window (start and end dates).

5. STACKING
-----------
Coupon codes generally cannot be stacked with other discount coupons. However, bank offers and cashback promotions may be combinable with coupon codes. Check the specific offer terms.

6. NON-TRANSFERABLE
-------------------
Promotional codes are non-transferable and may only be used by the account they were issued to. Misuse or resale of coupon codes is grounds for account suspension.

7. EXPIRED/INVALID CODES
-------------------------
Cartly is not responsible for expired codes or codes not applied correctly. If a valid code fails to apply, contact support before placing the order.

8. FLASH SALE POLICIES
-----------------------
Flash sale products are sold at discounted prices for a limited time window. These orders cannot be cancelled after 30 minutes. Returns follow standard return policy, but exchanges may be restricted.
