CARTLY E-COMMERCE PLATFORM — EXCHANGE POLICY
=============================================

Last Updated: January 2025

1. OVERVIEW
-----------
Cartly offers a product exchange option as an alternative to returns and refunds. Exchanges are available for eligible products where customers wish to receive a replacement of a different size, color, or variant.

2. EXCHANGE ELIGIBILITY
------------------------
Exchanges are available for:
- Apparel and footwear: Size or color exchanges within 15 days of delivery.
- Electronics: Same model exchange if the received unit is defective (within 7 days).
- Home goods: Exchange for different variants within 10 days.

Exchanges are not available for:
- Products listed as "Non-Exchangeable" on the product page.
- Items showing signs of use or damage by the customer.
- Products that are part of flash sale or clearance promotions.

3. HOW TO REQUEST AN EXCHANGE
------------------------------
1. Go to My Orders and select the order.
2. Click "Exchange Item" and select your reason.
3. Choose the new size, color, or variant from the available options.
4. Schedule reverse pickup. The original item is collected and the replacement dispatched simultaneously where possible.

4. EXCHANGE TIMELINE
---------------------
- Reverse pickup: 2–3 business days.
- QC of returned item: 1–2 business days.
- Dispatch of exchange item: Within 24 hours of QC clearance.
- Delivery of exchange item: Standard delivery timelines apply.

Total exchange turnaround: 7–10 business days.

5. PRICE DIFFERENCE
--------------------
- If the exchange item costs more than the original, the customer pays the difference.
- If the exchange item costs less, the difference is credited to the Cartly Wallet.

6. EXCHANGE LIMIT
-----------------
Each product can be exchanged only once. If the exchanged item also has an issue, a return and refund will be processed instead.

7. SELLER EXCHANGE POLICIES
-----------------------------
Third-party sellers may have different exchange windows. Always check the product page for seller-specific exchange terms. Cartly's standard policy acts as a minimum guarantee.
