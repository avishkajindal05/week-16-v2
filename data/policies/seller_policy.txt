CARTLY E-COMMERCE PLATFORM — SELLER POLICY
===========================================

Last Updated: January 2025

1. OVERVIEW
-----------
Cartly operates as a marketplace connecting third-party sellers with customers. This policy outlines the obligations, rights, and standards sellers must adhere to when operating on the Cartly platform.

2. SELLER REGISTRATION
-----------------------
To become a Cartly seller:
- Valid GSTIN is mandatory.
- Bank account details for payment settlement are required.
- PAN card verification is required for sellers above ₹20 lakh annual turnover.
- Product category approval may be required for regulated categories.

3. PRODUCT LISTINGS
--------------------
Sellers are responsible for:
- Accurate product descriptions, images, and specifications.
- Honest pricing (MRP must match the physical product label).
- Maintaining up-to-date inventory to avoid overselling.
- Compliance with BIS, FSSAI, and other regulatory standards for applicable categories.

4. ORDER FULFILMENT
--------------------
Sellers must:
- Accept or reject orders within 12 hours of placement.
- Dispatch accepted orders within 24–48 hours.
- Pack items securely to prevent transit damage.
- Upload tracking details to the Cartly Seller Portal immediately after dispatch.

5. SELLER PERFORMANCE STANDARDS
---------------------------------
Cartly monitors:
- Order Defect Rate (ODR): Must remain below 1%.
- Late Dispatch Rate: Must remain below 5%.
- Cancellation Rate: Must remain below 3%.

Sellers failing to meet performance standards face listing suspension or account deactivation.

6. PAYMENT SETTLEMENT
----------------------
Seller payments are settled on a weekly basis (every Thursday) for orders delivered and past the return window. Settlement may be withheld for orders under dispute.

7. PROHIBITED ITEMS
--------------------
Sellers may not list: counterfeit goods, weapons, narcotics, live animals, or items prohibited under Indian law. Violations result in immediate account termination and legal action.
